Sherpa

Learning made easy

We're bringing quality teaching and learning resources together, all in one place.

Sherpa is a one-stop learning platform with a global database of educational resources for the student and educators of the internet age. The progress of individual learners is also tracked through, and customised worksheets and tests are generated for them.

Through making education simple and affordable, we hope to empower individuals to take the first steps in their education journey, no matter where they are in the world.

Comprehensive

Sherpa’s database covers the entire range of secondary and pre-university level topics in mathematics and the sciences

Seamless

Our platform is easily navigable with the resources sorted according to each user’s learning profile e.g. high school student from Indonesia

Progressive

With customised worksheets and tests, individuals will be able to learn at their own pace and track their own progress

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible and high quality education materials for all learners and educators in the world.

Our Vision

Globally empowering educators and self- educators with equal access to high quality tools and teaching materials to unlock quality education for all.

Our Team

We are a team of 6 university students from who are very privileged to have gone through the Singapore education system and are concurrently studying in Singapore University of Design (SUTD) and Singapore Management University (SMU). We strongly believe in the value of a quality education, and that with access to the right resources and materials, anyone will be able to learn or to teach others.
