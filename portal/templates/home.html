Sherpa

Learning made easy

We're bringing quality teaching and learning resources together, all in one place



What is Sherpa?

We are building a one-stop, global database or learning resources and teaching materials for the students and educators of the internet age, making it simple and affordable for parents and education providers to teach structured, quality content to the next generation.

By empowering individuals around the world with easy access to quality learning and teaching materials, we want to massively reduce the opportunity cost and barriers of entry to teaching higher level education. This will make it easy for individuals to take the first steps in their education journey, no matter where they are in the world.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible and high quality education materials for all learners and educators in the world.

Our Vision

Globally empowering educators and self- educators with equal access to high quality tools and teaching materials to unlock quality education for all.
